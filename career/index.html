Careers

The following careers are available in Data Science, along with their yearly salaries (in USD!)
  • Data Analyst - $65,246
  • Database Administrator - $89,937
  • Computer Systems Analyst - $82,274
  • Machine Learning Engineer - $112,421
  • Data Engineer - $115,886

(Indeed)

There are often no exact 'Data Scientist' jobs just as there are no 'Computer Scientist' jobs; the term is so broad that you can either tinker with data, analyse databases or work with AI Engineers to create AI models.

Becoming a Data Scientist

Jobs, like the ones listed above, will often require a Computer Science or Data Science degree. Most universities will offer a Computer Science degree while offering a Data Science specialization with the degree. Here are a few examples;
  • University of Waterloo - Data Science (Bachelor of Computer Science OR Bachelor of Math in Data Science)
  • University of Toronto - Data Science (Certificate)
  • University of Ottawa - Data Science (Bachelor of Science in Mathematics AND Bachelor of Science in Computer Science)
Most universities will require a grade 12 english and a few math credits (often up to Pre-Calculus) in order to be admitted in these programs.
Make sure you check the requirements WELL in advance for any program you're interested in!
