What is Data Science?

Data Science is the integration of math, statistics, artificial intelligence (AI) and programming in Computer Science to compile and use data for any particular reason. (IBM)
In short, it is the procedure of taking large clusters of information/data and making sense of it using concepts within mathematics (i.e. statistics) and Computer Science (algorithms).

For example...

a data scientist might...
  • gather data about a viral outbreak to direct medical resources to certain areas
  • gather data about the userbase of a company to create products targeted to the users
  • compile data sets to train an AI

Could you think of any other things a data scientist might do?
